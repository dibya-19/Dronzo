Safety Guidelines

Your safety and secure deliveries are our top priority.

Do’s ✅

  • Keep a clear 3m x 3m landing zone.
  • Follow app prompts during drop & pickup.
  • Supervise children and pets during landing.
  • Report obstacles (wires, trees) in the app.

Don’ts ❌

  • Do not reach for the parcel until drone is stationary.
  • Do not throw objects at drones.
  • Do not attach extra weight to the package.
  • Do not enter restricted areas or rooftops unsafely.

Weather & No-Fly Conditions

Deliveries may pause during heavy rain, high winds, low visibility, or ATC advisories. You’ll get instant updates in the app.

Package Handling

Our stabilizers minimize sway. Wait for the green signal before removing the parcel. Fragile items include extra padding.

Emergency & Support

If there’s an incident, move to a safe distance and contact support via Contact Us.
