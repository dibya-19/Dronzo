Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about DronZo.

How does DronZo delivery work?

Place an order, choose a safe landing zone, and track the drone in real time. You’ll be notified at every step.

Which areas are covered?

We’re expanding city by city. Check availability in the app or website before placing an order.

How is pricing calculated?

Pricing depends on distance, package weight, and priority. Final price is shown before you confirm.

What affects ETA?

Weather, airspace advisories, and route distance can impact ETA. You’ll see live updates on the tracking page.

What can’t be delivered?

Hazardous materials, illegal items, living animals, and items exceeding max weight/dimensions.
